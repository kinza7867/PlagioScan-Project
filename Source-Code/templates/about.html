✨ About PlagioScan

Empowering originality through advanced plagiarism detection technology

📖 Our Story

Founded in 2024, PlagioScan was born from a simple yet powerful idea: make professional plagiarism detection accessible to everyone. We saw students struggling with unintentional plagiarism, educators spending countless hours checking submissions, and content creators worried about originality.

Today, we're proud to serve over 50,000 users worldwide, helping them maintain integrity and originality in their work.

🎯 Our Mission

To provide accurate, fast, and affordable plagiarism detection that empowers creators, students, and professionals to produce original content with confidence.

We believe that every piece of content deserves to be authentic, and our technology makes it possible for everyone to check and verify originality instantly.
