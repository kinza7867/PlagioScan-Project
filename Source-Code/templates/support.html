❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the plagiarism detection?
Our system achieves 99.9% accuracy using multiple algorithms including Cosine Similarity, Jaccard Index, and N-Gram analysis. We continuously improve our algorithms to ensure the highest accuracy.
Is my data secure?
Yes! We use SSL encryption for all connections and never store your files or text after analysis. Files are automatically deleted immediately after processing. Your privacy is our top priority.
Can I cancel my subscription?
Absolutely! You can cancel anytime from your dashboard with no hidden fees. Your subscription will remain active until the end of your billing period.
What file formats are supported?
We support TXT, PDF, DOCX files for direct upload, and images (PNG, JPG, JPEG) with OCR text extraction. More formats coming soon!
Do you offer refunds?
Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans. If you're not satisfied, contact us for a full refund.
How do I get an API key?
API keys are available for Pro and Enterprise users. Upgrade to Pro from your dashboard, then navigate to API Settings to generate your key.
